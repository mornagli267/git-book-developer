# SFP Modules

Various SolidRun products feature SFP/QSFP connectors.  
Module support varies widely between platforms and implementations, due to different implementations. I.e. Linux ARM systems rely on kernel drivers, whereas PCI-E NICs (and many x86 systems) use integrated firmware.

This page collects information on modules tested at SolidRun with a variety of boards.
