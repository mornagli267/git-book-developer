# AMD

### SolidRun AMD Ryzen Embedded platforms

SolidRun products based on **AMD Ryzen Embedded** processors. This includes **COM Express** (Type 6 / Type 7), **system-on-module (SoM)**, and **SBC / mini-ITX** platforms. These designs target edge compute and industrial embedded systems.
